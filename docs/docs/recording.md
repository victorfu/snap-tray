## Recording entry points

- Tray menu: Record Full Screen
- Region capture toolbar: press `R`

## Recording lifecycle

1. Pick region or full-screen target.
2. Click Start.
3. Use floating control bar to Pause/Resume.
4. Click Stop to export.

## Output formats

| Format | Typical use |
|---|---|
| MP4 (H.264) | Tutorials, long recordings |
| GIF | Short looping demos |
| WebP | Lightweight animated snippets |

## Audio options

Depending on platform support:

- Microphone
- System audio
- Mixed capture

## Quality tuning

Open Settings > Recording and adjust frame rate, quality, countdown, and preview behavior.
