Features

◥

Region Capture

Capture any area of your screen with precision crosshair and magnifier. Press F2 to start capturing.

✎

Screen Canvas

Draw annotations directly on your screen. Double-tap F2 to enter canvas mode.

📌

Pin to Screen

Keep screenshots floating on top of other windows. Resize and rotate pinned screenshots.

🖌

Rich Annotations

Arrow, pencil, marker, rectangle, text, mosaic, and step badges for professional annotations.

🔍

OCR Text Recognition

Extract text from any part of your screen with built-in OCR on macOS and Windows.

🗂

Window Detection

Automatically detect and select windows for quick capture.

🎨

Color Picker

Pick colors from anywhere on screen. Toggle HEX/RGB with Shift, copy with C.

↺

Undo/Redo

Full undo and redo support for all your annotations. Never lose your work.
